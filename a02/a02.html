Sincerely, Kentrell

YoungBoy Never Broke Again
YoungBoy Never Broke Again continued his run of chart-topping albums with 2021's Sincerely, Kentrell, his third official full-length album and fourth Billboard 200 number one. Preceded by five Hot 100-charting singles, including the Top 40 hit "On My Side," the album is filled with confessional, soul-baring tracks about violence, jail time, and his quest for redemption.

No guest rappers are featured, making the lyrics as pure an expression of YoungBoy's feelings as possible, while the huge cast of producers includes Mike WiLL Made-It, Smash David, and Einer Bankz. Sincerely, Kentrell is a record that is sure to be a hit with fans and critics alike.
